About Us

Architecture service

Architectural Metal & Glazing Works

At Arab Metal, we specialize in delivering high-quality architectural metal and glazing solutions, combining innovation, durability, and aesthetic appeal. Our expertise spans across a diverse range of custom-engineered metal and glass applications tailored to meet modern architectural and interior design needs.

Metal Works

We offer precision-engineered metal fabrication solutions, ensuring strength, functionality, and design excellence:

    • • Handrails & Balustrades – Sleek stainless steel, mild steel, and glass balustrade systems.
      • Staircases & Ladders – Custom steel staircases, spiral staircases, and emergency escape ladders.
      • Fences & Gates – Secure and stylish fences and gates in steel, stainless steel, and aluminum.
      • Mashrabiya & Decorative Screens – Intricately designed stainless steel, corten steel, and aluminum screens.
      • Column & Lift Cladding – Elegant stainless steel, aluminum, and PVDF-coated panel cladding.
      • Steel Structures – Custom-fabricated pergolas, canopies, and retractable shades.
      • Bollards & Barriers – Fixed, removable, and automatic bollard solutions.
      • Expansion Joints & Gratings – Heavy-duty steel grating, stainless steel mesh, and cover plates.

    • Glazing Works

  • Our glazing solutions provide modern, high-performance glass systems for commercial and residential applications:
      • Glass Partitions & Enclosures – Office partitions, demountable partitions, and shower enclosures.
      • Fire-Rated Glazed Systems – Fire-resistant glass doors, windows, and curtain walls.
      • Curtain Wall Systems – Structural glazing, unitized and semi-unitized façades.
      • Glass Canopies & Skylights – Overhead glazing solutions for natural lighting and aesthetics.
      • Glass Handrails & Balustrades – Frameless and framed glass railing systems for safety and style.
      • Shopfronts & Automatic Doors – Modern, frameless glass storefronts and sensor-operated entrances.
      • Bespoke Architectural Features – Custom metal-and-glass design elements tailored to unique projects.

    • Retractable Shades & Canopies


    Enhance outdoor spaces with Arab Metal’s premium retractable shades and canopies, designed for style, functionality, and durability. Our shading solutions provide protection from the sun, heat, and rain while adding a sleek architectural touch to residential, commercial, and hospitality spaces.

      ✔ Pergolas – Modern and classic designs with integrated lighting and automation
